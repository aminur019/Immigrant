How It Works

Step 1: Submit Your Application

Step 2: Get Verified

Step 3: Achieve Citizenship

Step 1: Submit Your Application

Fill out the online application form with your personal details and required documents. Make sure to provide accurate information to avoid any delays in the process. You will need to upload scanned copies of your identification, proof of residency, and other relevant documents. Once submitted, you will receive a confirmation email with your application number.

Step 2: Get Verified

Our team will review your application and verify your documents. This process may take a few days. During this time, we may contact you for additional information or clarification. Once your documents are verified, you will receive an email notification confirming your verification status. If any issues are found, you will be guided on how to resolve them.

Step 3: Achieve Citizenship

Once verified, you will receive guidance on the next steps to achieve your US citizenship. This includes preparing for the citizenship test, attending interviews, and completing any additional paperwork. Our team will provide you with resources and support to help you succeed. After completing all the requirements, you will be invited to take the Oath of Allegiance and officially become a US citizen.
